Jun 20 Sat · Chandratal Lake 🌙
⚠️ Pre-check: Road open?
Check live status at spitivalleypackages.com the previous night. Kunzum Pass → Batal → Chandratal diversion opens mid-June.
🌅 4 AM — Depart Manali
Manali → Gramphu → Batal → Chandratal. ~130 km, 5–6 hrs with rough terrain.
Full tank + extra fuel. Carry water, warm layers, energy bars.
🏕️ Camp at Chandratal
Stay at seasonal camps 2 km from lake (lakeside camping banned). ₹1,200–1,500/person.
Get FREE e-permit at eaagman.hp.gov.in before leaving home. Entry ₹150/person.
🌙 Sunset at the Lake
The crescent-moon shaped lake at 4,300m. One of India's most stunning sights. Worth every penny!
